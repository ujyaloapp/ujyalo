Why we started Ujyalo, what we believe in, and where we're going.

Why Ujyalo exists

Every year, hundreds of thousands of Nepali students sit for the SEE exam. For most of them, this single exam shapes the rest of their lives — which college they attend, which career they pursue, which opportunities open up.

But preparation for SEE in Nepal hasn't really changed in decades. Expensive coaching centers. Thick guidebooks. Photocopied past papers. Long waits to get answers from teachers.

Meanwhile, the world has access to incredible AI technology. Why shouldn't a student in Pokhara have the same advantage as a student in Singapore?

That's why we built Ujyalo — to put a patient, knowledgeable teacher in every Nepali student's pocket.

What we believe

🎯 Every student deserves a great teacher

Geography shouldn't decide your future. AI lets us bring quality teaching to every corner of Nepal — from Kathmandu to Karnali.

💸 Affordable matters

Coaching centers cost Rs 5,000–15,000 per month. We made Ujyalo Rs 299 because access to good education shouldn't depend on family wealth.

🇳🇵 Built for Nepal, not adapted

Other tools are American products with Nepali stickers. Ujyalo is built from the ground up for the Nepal SEE syllabus, with Nepali context in every question.

👨‍👩‍👧 Parents matter too

Most Nepali parents pay for their child's education. They deserve to see what they're paying for. Weekly reports keep them informed and involved.

Where we're going.

Our goal: become the trusted study companion for every Nepali student preparing for SEE, NEB, and entrance exams.

We're starting with SEE because it's the most important exam in a Nepali student's life. From there — NEB Grade 11/12, then IOE/CMAT entrance exams, then medical entrance.

One bright future at a time.
