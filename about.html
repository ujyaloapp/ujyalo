Why Ujyalo exists

The pressure is real.

Every year, hundreds of thousands of Nepali students sit for the SEE exam carrying the weight of their entire family on their shoulders.

It is not just an exam. It is pride. It is honour. It is the thing parents talk about at family gatherings and dream about quietly at night.

Most families save money for tuition, buy thick guidebooks, make sacrifices. But even expensive coaching centres do not always deliver. And for families who cannot afford them? Their child faces Nepal's most important exam essentially alone.

"Where you are born should not decide how far you go."

That is the problem Ujyalo was built to solve. A patient, always-available study companion that knows the SEE inside out. Free to start. Built for Nepal. Available 24 hours a day.
