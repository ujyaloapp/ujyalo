Our story

ujyalo means light.
We're here to share it.

A small team on a simple mission: give every Nepali student world-class exam preparation, free — no matter where they live or what they can afford.

Across Nepal, students preparing for the SEE face the same struggle: scattered past papers, no clear answers, and the best help locked behind fees.

Good preparation too often depends on where you live or what your family can afford. We didn't think that was fair.

So we set out to gather SEE papers, write honest model answers, and make it all free — in Nepali and English — in a space that feels calm and good to study in.

Where you are born should not decide how far you go.

— The ujyalo team
