Privacy policy.

Last updated: 7 May 2026. We take your privacy seriously.

This is a simplified summary. Plain language, no legal jargon.

What we collect

When you sign up, we collect your name, school, grade, email, and (optionally) your parent's email. When you practice, we save your answers and progress.

That's it. We don't track you across the web. We don't collect data we don't need.

How we use it

  • To give you a personalised experience (your dashboard, progress, weak chapters).
  • To send weekly reports to your parent (if you opt in).
  • To improve Ujyalo (we look at usage patterns, never individual data).
  • To send you helpful emails about new features (you can opt out anytime).

What we never do

  • ❌ We never sell your data. To anyone. Ever.
  • ❌ We never share your data with advertisers.
  • ❌ We never read your individual answers (we look at aggregate patterns only).
  • ❌ We never share data without your consent.

AI processing

When you submit an answer, it's sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) for evaluation. They process the answer to give feedback, then it's deleted. They don't store your data or use it for training.

Your rights

  • ✓ See all your data anytime in your profile.
  • ✓ Download your data — email us and we'll send it.
  • ✓ Delete your account — instant, complete erasure.
  • ✓ Opt out of emails anytime.

Children's privacy

Ujyalo is built for students Grade 10+ (typically 14-16 years old). For students under 13, parent consent is required to create an account.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we'll email you. You'll always know what's happening.

Questions?

Email hello@ujyalo.app. We respond to every email.
