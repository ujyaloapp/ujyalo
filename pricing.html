Can I cancel anytime?
+
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your Student subscription anytime from your profile page. No questions asked. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
How do I pay in Nepal?
+
We accept eSewa, Khalti, and bank transfer. International students can also pay with credit/debit card. Coming soon — we'll announce when payment is ready.
What's included in the free plan?
+
The free plan gives you 10 AI-generated questions per day across two subjects of your choice. Perfect for testing the product before committing. Most students upgrade after 1–2 weeks.
Do you offer scholarships?
+
Yes — students from underprivileged backgrounds can apply for free Student-tier access. Email us at hello@ujyalo.app with your school principal's recommendation.
