Practice is how real learning happens.

Reading your textbook is important. But answering questions is how you actually understand — and remember.

When you try to answer a question — even if you get it wrong — your brain learns far more than reading the same paragraph 10 times.

Waiting until the next class to find out you got something wrong means you practice the mistake. Instant model answers fix that.
