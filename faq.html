FAQ

Frequently asked
questions.

Everything students and parents commonly ask about Ujyalo. Can't find what you're looking for? Get in touch.

For students
Is Ujyalo free?
+
Yes — completely free to start. The free plan gives 10 questions per day across two subjects. Upgrade only if you want unlimited practice and all features.
Will Ujyalo help me pass SEE?
+
Ujyalo gives you the tools — past papers, mock tests, instant feedback, progress tracking. But you have to put in the work. Students who practice 30 minutes daily for 2–3 months see major improvement.
What if I don't have internet always?
+
Ujyalo works on slow internet — we designed it to be lightweight. You only need internet when generating new questions or submitting answers. Practice during your school's free WiFi or anywhere with mobile data.
Is the AI in Nepali or English?
+
All AI responses are in clear, simple English. Why? Because Nepali AI quality isn't good enough yet. Plus, your SEE exam is mostly in English anyway. We use Nepali context (names, places, currency) but the language is English.
Can I use it on my phone?
+
Absolutely. Ujyalo works perfectly on phones, tablets, and computers. Most of our students study on their phones. No app to download — just open ujyalo.app in your browser.
For parents
How will I know my child is studying?
+
Every Sunday morning, you receive a beautiful email showing exactly what your child practised that week. How many questions, accuracy, weak chapters, and streaks. You see real progress, not just promises.
Is Rs 299/month worth it?
+
Coaching centers cost Rs 5,000–15,000 per month. Tutors cost Rs 2,000–5,000. Ujyalo is Rs 299 per month and works 24/7. Try the free plan first — most parents see the value clearly within 2 weeks.
Is my child's data safe?
+
Yes. We never share data with third parties. We don't sell information. Your child's practice history is theirs alone. See our privacy policy for full details.
For schools
Can our school use Ujyalo for all students?
+
Yes — we offer School plans starting Rs 5,000/month for up to 30 students. Includes teacher dashboard, class progress reports, and priority support. Email hello@ujyalo.app for a demo.
Do you offer custom question banks?
+
Yes — schools can upload their own question banks for students to practice. Useful for teachers who want to align practice with what they're teaching in class.
